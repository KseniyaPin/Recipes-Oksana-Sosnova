  • For a caramel cake you will need a can of boiled condensed milk. Make the layers biscuit-like, they turn out fluffy and soft and very pleasant to the taste. The cream for such a cake is butter with condensed milk. Grease each layer and leave it in the refrigerator overnight.

  • Form eclairs from choux pastry. Bake eclairs in an oven preheated to 180-200 degrees for about 30-35 minutes. Cool. You can fill eclairs with custard cream, butter cream or whipped cream with sugar.

  • Bake the chocolate sponge cake in the oven for about 45-50 minutes. Cool the cake and cut it into 2 parts. Spread the sponge cake with an even layer of apricot jam. Coat the cake with melted chocolate on all sides. In this form, put the cake in the refrigerator for 2 hours, then you can eat it.
